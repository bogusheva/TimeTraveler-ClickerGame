The most wicked and malevolent villain of all times has set a new plan in motion to conquer the world. He has discovered the ability to travel through time and is using this power to gather all the villains from history to join his cause.

You are the Time Traveler, a brave warrior who has the ability to travel through time and put an end to the evil plan. Your mission is to find and eliminate all the villains from history by clicking on them, with each click taking away a point from their health. So they won't help the most evil villain in out time.

The fate of the world is in your hands. The battle between good and evil has intensified, and only you can prevent the ultimate victory of the forces of darkness. Are you ready to save the world?
